About The Event

DDU-GKY (Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana) is a national placement-linked skill training program for rural youth aged 15-35 from poor families, launched by India's Ministry of Rural Development to enhance skills for wage employment and diversify income sources. The scheme provides high-quality, demand-driven training in various sectors, focusing on job roles and ensuring post-placement tracking and career progression. DDU-GKY is a part of the National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM) and aims to contribute to initiatives like Skill India and Make in India.
